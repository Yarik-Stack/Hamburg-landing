This website is about Hamburg

Hamburg is one of the largest port cities in Europe, located at the confluence of the Elbe River and the North Sea.

Some facts about the city

Hamburg is one of the largest ports in Europe. Hundreds of different types of ships pass through the port every day, both cruise ships and multi-ton tankers.

Since Hamburg is one of the largest cities in Germany, its central station is a very busy place with high passenger traffic.

Hamburg has some of the most beautiful architecture in all of Germany.

Historically, Hamburg is a city on the water. In the center of the city there are two large lakes and the city itself stands on the Elba River. Waterfowl can be found in many places in the city.

Hamburg is a city with many facets and numerous sights. From the harbor to the Reeperbahn and the Alster to the city park, there is a lot to see. Hamburg is maritime, Hamburg is green, Hamburg is historic - discover the most beautiful corners of the Hanseatic city.
